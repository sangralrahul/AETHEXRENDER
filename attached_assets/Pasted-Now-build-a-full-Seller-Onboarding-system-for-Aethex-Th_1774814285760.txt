Now build a full Seller Onboarding system for Aethex. This allows medical brands and suppliers to list and sell their own products on Aethex (like Amazon seller). Here is everything needed:

---

1. SELLER REGISTRATION (/seller/register)
- Separate seller signup form (not regular user signup)
- Fields required:
  * Business name
  * Owner name
  * Business email + phone
  * GST number (mandatory for India)
  * Business category (Surgical, Diagnostics, Pharma, PPE, Equipment, etc.)
  * Business address + pincode
  * Bank account details for payouts (account number, IFSC, account holder name)
  * Upload: GST certificate, PAN card, business registration proof
- After submission: "Your seller application is under review. We will contact you within 48 hours."
- Admin gets notified of new seller application

---

2. SELLER APPROVAL (Admin Panel)
- New seller applications listed under Admin > Sellers > Pending
- Admin can: Approve, Reject with reason, Request more documents
- On approval: seller gets email with login credentials for seller dashboard
- On rejection: seller gets email with reason

---

3. SELLER DASHBOARD (/seller/dashboard)
- Overview: total products listed, total orders, total revenue, pending payouts
- Quick stats: this month sales, top selling product, average rating
- Sidebar: Dashboard, Products, Orders, Payouts, Analytics, Settings

---

4. SELLER PRODUCT MANAGEMENT
- Add new product: name, description, category, price, MRP, GST%, stock, images (up to 5), specifications, shipping weight
- Products go to Pending review before going live on Aethex
- Edit/delete existing products
- See product performance: views, add to cart count, sales
- Low stock alerts (when stock < 10)

---

5. SELLER ORDER MANAGEMENT
- See all orders for their products only
- Accept or reject incoming orders
- Mark as shipped with courier name + tracking number
- View buyer delivery address
- Download packing slip PDF for each order

---

6. PAYOUTS
- Aethex takes 10% commission on every sale (show this clearly)
- Remaining 90% goes to seller
- Payouts processed every 7 days automatically via Razorpay Route or manual bank transfer
- Payout history page: date, amount, status (paid/pending)
- Minimum payout threshold: ₹500

---

7. SELLER ANALYTICS
- Revenue chart: daily/weekly/monthly
- Top products by sales and revenue
- Order status breakdown
- Customer locations (state-wise)

---

8. SELLER SETTINGS
- Edit business profile
- Update bank details
- Shipping settings: which pincodes they deliver to, delivery time estimate
- Vacation mode: temporarily hide all their products

---

9. SELLER BADGE ON PRODUCT PAGES
- Each product shows seller name + rating on product detail page
- Clicking seller name shows their mini storefront with all their products
- Top sellers get a Verified Seller badge

---
